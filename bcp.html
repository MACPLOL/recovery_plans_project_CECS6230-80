Business Continuity Plan (BCP)

A Business Continuity Plan (BCP) ensures critical business operations continue during and after disruptive events. It covers IT systems as well as processes like human resources, supply chain, and communications.

Key Components:

  • Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
  • Continuity Strategies (Alternate Sites, Remote Work, etc.)
  • Crisis Management & Communication Plan
  • Organizational Roles & Hierarchy
  • Testing & Training

Importance to IT Auditing: Auditors check if the BCP is comprehensive, regularly updated, and integrates with the DRP. They ensure compliance with standards like ISO 22301.
