Welcome to My Recovery Plans Comparison Blog

This blog is dedicated to exploring three essential components of organizational resilience: Disaster Recovery Plans (DRP), Business Continuity Plans (BCP), and Incident Response Plans (IRP).

Through these pages, you will find detailed insights and master’s-level analyses on how each plan functions, how they interrelate, and why they are critical to both IT auditing and overall organizational success. We also highlight best practices from multiple sources, including guidance from NIST, FFIEC, and real-world experts.

Please use the navigation links above to learn more about each plan individually, compare them side-by-side, or read about the context of this assignment in the “About Me” section.

This blog is part of Assignment 2, Part V, for the CECS 6230-80 course at the Polytechnic University of Puerto Rico, under the guidance of Professor Dr. Cruz.
