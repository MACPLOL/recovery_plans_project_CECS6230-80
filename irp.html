Incident Response Plan (IRP)

An Incident Response Plan details how to detect, respond to, and recover from security incidents (e.g., data breaches, malware attacks).

Key Components:

  • Incident Detection & Monitoring
  • Roles & Responsibilities (Security Team, Management, etc.)
  • Containment, Eradication, & Recovery Processes
  • Communication & Escalation Procedures
  • Post-Incident Review & Lessons Learned

Importance to IT Auditing: Auditors review whether the IRP is well-documented, tested, and whether the organization learns from incidents to improve security posture.
